How it works

How does it work?

The hunt is simple. Follow these 4 steps and let the clues guide you. Happy hunting.

01
The Booking

Choose a Treasure Hunt

Find an upcoming Treasure Hunt in your city and secure a ticket. Hunts are fixed date/location experiences — you pick when and where, and we handle the rest.

Hunts only launch once the minimum number of players is reached. If we don't hit the minimum, your ticket is refunded automatically — no action needed from your side.

89 DKK per hunter 2–4 hours Refund if not unlocked

02
The Unlock

The Hunt Unlocks

Once enough hunters join, the passage opens. You'll receive an e-mail with your start time, how your first clue will land, and what to bring (spoiler: a charged phone and comfortable shoes).

As more hunters join after you, the prize grows — so there's no penalty for booking early.

E-mail confirmation Start time revealed Prize grows with hunters
03
The Trail

Follow the Clues

Your first clue arrives by e-mail at the start time. Clues are designed to be solved in the real world, with digital elements — think "decode this, walk there, look around."

All clue locations are in public, accessible places. Bring a smartphone, logic, and observational skills. No prior knowledge required — solo or with friends.

Smartphone required Public locations No prior knowledge Solo or team
04
The Prize

Find the Treasure

The first hunter to reach the treasure claims it. Additional rewards may vary per hunt. Prize value scales with participation — the bigger the hunt, the bigger the pot.

Winners take the cash. Everyone else takes the story.

700 DKK base prize First to reach wins Rewards may vary
Common questions

Before you book.

How many hunters does a hunt need to unlock?

A minimum is set per hunt so there's always enough competition. The Aarhus pop-up unlocks at 10 hunters; the monthly Copenhagen hunt is unlocked by default. You only pay if it runs.

What if it rains?

Hunts run rain or shine. Clue locations are chosen with weather in mind — no hiking, no rooftops. Dress for a walk, not a hike.

Can I join with kids?

Absolutely — kids love it, and clues are playable with a family. One ticket per hunter who wants to play; younger kids tag along free.

How long does the hunt actually take?

Most hunters finish in 2–4 hours. The winner usually clocks in closer to 2; casual teams take the scenic route and stay out for 4.

Ready to hunt?

Pick your city, secure your spot, and wait for the first clue to land in your inbox.

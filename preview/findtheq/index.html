Common questions

Before you book.

How do I actually get the first clue?

By e-mail, at the start time (13:00 on hunt day). You solve it on your phone, then go find the real-world location.

What if the hunt doesn't unlock?

Hunts only launch once enough hunters have joined. If we don't hit the minimum, your 89 DKK is refunded — no action needed from your side.

Do I need to be clever at riddles?

No prior knowledge required. Clues reward logic, observation, and willingness to move. A smartphone is enough.

Can I hunt as a team?

Yes — come alone or with friends. The ticket is per hunter; bigger teams just mean more brains on the clue.
